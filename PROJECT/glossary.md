# Glossary

Quick reference for abbreviations and terms used throughout the course.

---

## Hydrogeological Parameters

| Symbol | Term | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| **K** | Hydraulic conductivity | Measure of how easily water flows through a porous medium [m/d or m/s] |
| **Ss** | Specific storage | Volume of water released per unit volume of aquifer per unit decline in head [1/m] |
| **Sy** | Specific yield | Volume of water released by gravity drainage per unit volume of aquifer [-] |
| **n** | Porosity | Fraction of void space in a porous medium [-] |
| **T** | Transmissivity | Product of hydraulic conductivity and aquifer thickness (T = K × b) [m²/d] |
| **h** | Hydraulic head | Energy per unit weight of water, expressed as elevation [m] |
| **q** | Specific discharge | Volumetric flow rate per unit area (Darcy flux) [m/d] |
| **R** | Recharge | Rate of water entering the aquifer from above [m/d or mm/yr] |

---

## Transport Parameters

| Symbol | Term | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| **αL** | Longitudinal dispersivity | Dispersion in the direction of flow [m] |
| **αT** | Transverse dispersivity | Dispersion perpendicular to flow [m] |
| **Rf** | Retardation factor | Ratio of groundwater velocity to solute velocity [-] |
| **Kd** | Distribution coefficient | Ratio of sorbed to dissolved concentration [L/kg] |

## Data & Technical Terms

| Abbreviation | Term | Description |
|--------------|------|-------------|
| **DEM** | Digital Elevation Model | Gridded representation of terrain elevation |
| **GIS** | Geographic Information System | Software for spatial data management and analysis |
| **MODFLOW** | Modular Groundwater Flow Model | USGS numerical groundwater flow simulation code |
| **MT3DMS** | Modular Transport 3D Multi-Species | Solute transport simulation code |
| **FloPy** | — | Python package for creating and running MODFLOW models |
